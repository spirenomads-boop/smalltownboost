Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation actually take?

+

Roughly 1 day if you're prepared and have a shop or fabrication experience. Professional installation is recommended for best results and peace of mind.

Will this fit my M13A?

+

Yes, it's specifically designed for the Suzuki Jimny M13A (2007+). Perfect fit, proven on multiple vehicles.

Is ECU tuning really necessary?

+

YES. Proper tuning is critical for reliability and performance. This kit is designed to work with 91 octane fuel and stock ECU at modest boost, but professional calibration is strongly recommended for safety and longevity.

Why the M65 and not a different supercharger?

+

The M65 is the sweet spot for the M13A. It offers the best balance of power, reliability, and drivability. Larger units add complexity; smaller ones don't deliver enough low-end torque.

How much power am I getting?

+

Expect significant low-end torque gains—exactly what Jimny owners need for rock crawling, trails, and daily driving. The M65 delivers consistent, usable power across the RPM range.

What's the reliability story?

+

The Eaton M65 is a proven OEM unit built to last. Seals and bearings rarely need servicing. When they do, rebuild kits are readily available and inexpensive.

Can I see it installed?

+

Absolutely. Check out the photo gallery and build/performance videos above. This is the real prototype on Travis's personal Jimny—you're seeing exactly what you'll get.

What if I'm not mechanically inclined?

+

No problem. We can connect you with shops that specialize in Jimny performance work. Professional installation is always the safest, smartest option.
